A light brown colour with yellowish spots, African reed frogs have slimy skin and large eyes. These frogs are native to sub-Saharan Africa. Argentine Horned Frog

A yellowish green colour with red stripes and brown spots the Argentine horned frog is native to Argentina. These large frogs have been known to live for as long as 10 years and grow to 18cm in length. They inhabit rain forest areas and their diet consists of rodents such as mice, small reptiles, spiders and locusts although they will try to eat anything they can fit in their mouth.
